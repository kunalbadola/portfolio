I’m a visual identity designer focused on building clear, structured brand systems for modern businesses. I work independently with founders and teams, helping them define and express who they are through purposeful visual systems that work across digital and physical touchpoints.

My approach is grounded in understanding the business first its goals, context, audience, and how design can support growth over time. I treat visual identity as a system rather than isolated assets; the result is work that is considered, consistent, and built to last.

Over the years, I’ve collaborated with brands at different stages, from early-stage ventures to growing companies that want clarity and consistency in how they present themselves to the world. I take on a limited number of projects to ensure focused attention and thoughtful execution for every engagement.

I believe design should solve problems, support decisions, and create clarity not just look attractive. Clear, practical, and usable visual identity is what I build, with purpose and discipline.

Work with me

Whether you’re looking to refine an existing logo or build a full visual identity system, I’m always open to thoughtful conversations.

An introductory email outlining your business and what you’re trying to achieve is ideal. If there’s a good fit, we can continue the discussion from there.
