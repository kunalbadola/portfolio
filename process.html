The steps below provide a brief overview of how I work. Each project begins with a clear understanding of your business and objectives. From there, we define the role design should play and make focused decisions to move the work forward.

Introduction

I begin with a conversation about your business, ambitions, and the challenge you need design to solve. This is not a formality, it’s how I understand your context and what success looks like to you. From this initial discussion, I share an indicative scope, timeline, and pricing range so we’re aligned before any commitment.

Proposal

Once agreed there’s a good fit, I prepare a detailed proposal. This document outlines deliverables, timelines, fixed-fee pricing, and terms. A 50% deposit typically initiates the project, with the remainder due before delivery of final files.

Research & Understanding

Good design starts with understanding. Here I explore your goals, audience, category, competitors, and brand history. I ask focused questions that clarify what you want to achieve, the context you’re operating in, and how success will be measured. The more precise the brief, the stronger the outcome.

Strategic Direction

Before drawing a single shape, I establish a strategic direction. This phase translates insight from research into clear visual principles. I often articulate 3 to 4 possible directions in words first, so we agree on a conceptual path before visuals begin.

Design Development

With strategy agreed, the design work begins. I typically produce one or two refined concepts that best answer the strategic brief. Each direction is shown with contextual mockups so you can see how the identity works in real environments from digital to print and physical applications. Feedback at this stage is constructive, focused on how well the work supports your goals.

Implementation & Delivery

All assets are prepared with real-world use in mind. That might include brand guidelines, templates, file systems for digital and print, and anything else you need to adopt the identity with confidence. Even after the project closes, I’m available to support further design needs or expansion of the system.
